IN SAFE HANDS?

ivy is a global hygiene solution company with a portfolio that ranges from paper products and guest-room amenities to natural and chemical cleaning solutions. We are based in Amman, Jordan, and our partnerships extend to a group of the biggest and the most innovative manufacturers around the globe.

Ivy has dedicated itself to understanding not only the varying types of hygiene products and solutions in the market, but also the hygiene needs of each of its customers. This knowledge has a lowed us to deliver solutions that add real value to our customers’ environments and communities in a way that is relevant to their work culture. This in turn has created a relationship among Ivy, its partners and its customers that is based on trust and understanding. ​ Ivy honors these relationships by continuously: ​ Sourcing the relevant and up-to-date products. Designing the product and its packaging that balance functionality and aesthetics, Delivering the product in a timely fashion, and Providing routine maintenance to ensure proper operation and safety. ​ ​ Ivy’s operation ends only when the customer has tested our product and found it successful and cost efficient.

OUR AIM.

Our aim is to provide solutions that enrich the work environment of our customers by providing a clean and healthy atmosphere that facilitates and stimulates growth. Our comprehensive hygiene solutions cater to hotels, hospitals, companies of all sizes, schools and educational institutions, manufacturers, and retailers. In addition to our supply network, we have an online shop that provides professional products and solutions for households and small-sized businesses.
